INSPIRATION TO START ATHIDHI ASHRAMAM:

THIS MONTH WE HAVE VISITED ATHIDHI ASHRAMAM LOCATED AT KUC CROSS ROAD,REDDY COLONY WARANGAL ,THIS ASHRAMAM WAS STARTED BY SUJATHA MAM 10 YEARS BACK WITH 20 MR CHILDREN,WHO LOST HER DAUGHTER WHO WAS 1 YEAR OLD AT THE RAILWAY STATION,SHE SEARCHED FOR MANY DAYS IN A HOPE OF FINDING  HER DAUGHTER,BUT COULDN’T FIND HER,AND SHE WENT INTO DEPRESSION FOR NEARLY 4 YEARS,AFTER LOOSING HOPE OF FINDING HER DAUGHTER,SHE DECIDED TO BECOME  MOTHER OF MENTALLY RETARDED(MR) CHILDREN,WHOM THE SOCIETY HAS FORGOTTEN.EVERYONE OF US HAS ONLY ONE LIFE,BEING MENTALLY RETARDED IS NOT BY THEIR CHOICE,THEY ARE BORN THAT WAY,LOOKING AFTER THE CHILDREN WHO DON’T FIT INTO THE SOCIETY NORMS ,SUJATHA MAM SEES THE SMILE OF HER LOST DAUGHTER IN THE 50 MR CHILDREN
